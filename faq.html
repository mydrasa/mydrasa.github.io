FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are mydrasa services?
    mydrasa offers six main services; assessments, AI mentor, courses and lessons, social networking, private tutoring, and finally goals and rewards. All these services are fully integrated to provide the student with a seamless journey and make his effort and time more productive.
  • What are the curricula covered?
    We are covering the IGCSE, SAT, IB, and the Egyptian National Curriculum. We are continuously adding more local curricula.
  • What are the school years covered?
    We are covering high school from grade 9-12. We are planning to add mid-school students by next year.
  • How my curriculum or school year becomes available?
    It is great to know that you are interested to join mydrasa. All you need to do is to register, mention your curriculum and school year, and once we receive enough registration for your curriculum or school year we will make it as a priority.
  • What is the AI mentor?
    AI mentor is an Artificial Intelligence tool that will transform your studying experience. Through the activities that you will be performing on the platform like the assessments, viewing lessons, and the social network activities, the AI mentor will be able to recognize your strengths, weakness, and interests, and gives you the most fit recommendations and directions.
  • How the AI mentor can support us?
    The AI mentor addresses the following metrics: Weaknesses; provide you with specific study recommendations to shortcut your studying effort and time. Strengths; promote your name in the social network as the best to support others in specific topic you excel in. Interests; show you topics that are related to your preference. Evaluations; supports you in the evaluation of essay type questions by correcting them and recommending the best answers.
  • How to find a lesson?
    You can search for any lesson using the search bar in your dashboard. You can search for it by name, tutor, or any keyword within the topic. You can also find lessons when you navigate through subjects, chapter, and sub chapters. You will find many lessons covering the same topic created by different tutors; check the lesson summary, lesson topics, and the intro video before enrolling into the lesson. If you are interested to view the lesson you can select "Enroll Now" and you can start viewing the lesson content.
  • What is a standard lesson?
    Standard Lesson: Lessons that are developed by tutors and available for free under the membership subscription.
  • What is a premium lesson?
    Premium Lesson: Lessons that are developed by tutors and viewed by students after paying the lesson price in mydrasa coins.
  • What is a course?
    Course is a complete set of lessons that covers the whole learning objectives of a subject.
  • What is mydrasa social network "MSN"?
    "MSN" is a social platform that enables users who share the same interests to get connected together. MSN will allow users to follow each other, post feeds, and receive comments. Students will be able to post questions or inquires; Tutors will be able to share their knowledge, post their latest updates including topics, answers, or upcoming events.
  • What is the quickest way I can get an answer for my question?
    Simply, post it on the social network, tag the relevant subject, tag followers, and you should receive comment on your post. The mydrasa AI mentor tool will see your post and will tag automatically the top students and tutors to contribute to your inquiry.
  • How to request a private tutoring support?
    Access the private tutoring service from your dashboard; select a tutor based on your preference from the top filters menus, check their profile, then schedule and pay your session instantly. There is no need for confirmations or waiting time. You could request your session on the same day.
  • What are the services under the membership subscription?
    Check out our pricing page to view all the services and benefits offered within our membership subscription.
  • What is mydrasa coin?
    mydrasa coin is the payment currency that mydrasa accept to pay any service or product available on the platform.
  • What are the benefits of using coins?
    mydrasa coins allows students to:

    Pay directly any service without the need to use a credit card. Simply parents can purchase coin packs once a month and students can spend them on studying whenever needed.

    Get rewarded in coins once you achieve your goals.

    Coins are transferable every month; differently from other subscriptions you don't lose what you have paid for, coins stay in the wallet and you can spend them the next month.
  • When do I need to use coins?
    Most of our services are offered for unlimited access as part of the membership subscription, however because we pay tutors for their lessons and tutoring services we require paying for these services in coins in order to pay them. This model allows you to pay only for the services you need rather than paying a full subscription package and you lose the value you paid at the end of the month.
  • How to earn mydrasa coins?
    mydrasa offers many ways that will allow you to win coins, prizes, and even international trips; complete challenges set by your parent, tutor, or mydrasa, invite students to follow you, help other students with solving problems, and you will be a winner for many prizes on the platform.
  • How to spend rewards?
    You can spend coins on any service or product offered on mydrasa platform. Through our partners, you will be able to convert your rewarded coins into gift cards and buy any item directly from their stores. For other prizes, you will get notified through your account to arrange for receiving them.
  • How to join mydrasa as a tutor?
    Visit mydrasa and create a tutor account. Our local partner will be in touch with you to complete the screening process before you start creating lessons and providing tutoring services.
  • What is mydrasa's commission rate?
    mydrasa takes a 30% commission on lessons and 15% on live tutoring sessions, so tutors earn 70% of lesson revenue and 85% of live tutoring revenue. This model rewards tutors fairly for their invaluable contribution.
